Red meat and large portions of meat
Hard on the kidneys · reduce portions significantly
Reduce▼
Why this mattersHis kidneys are already struggling. Protein from meat creates waste products (urea, creatinine) that the kidneys have to filter — the exact things that are elevated in his labs. Too much protein makes the kidneys work even harder and speeds up damage. His daily protein target is roughly 40–55g total for the whole day — one large serving of carne asada can already be 40–50g.
Avoid or significantly reduce:
